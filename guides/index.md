# Guides

The Anaplan API provides various [APIs](https://help.anaplan.com/anaplan-api-844c6d40-a21c-423d-8435-ebaaa0372b76) to interact with the Anaplan platform. This SDK supports all of them and their respective domains and use cases. Refer to the respective Guides to get started with each API.

- [**Bulk API**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/bulk/index.md) for large and efficient data ingress & egress
- [**Transactional API**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/transactional/index.md) for fine-grained manipulations of your models
- [**CloudWorks API**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/cloud_works/index.md) for orchestrating and automating your Anaplan processes
- [**ALM API**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/alm/index.md) for managing your Anaplan assets and their lifecycle
- [**Audit API**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/audit/index.md) for tracking changes and maintaining compliance
- [**Auth APIs**](https://vinzenzklass.github.io/anaplan-sdk/guides/authentication/index.md) are used internally by the SDK to automatically manage authentication.
